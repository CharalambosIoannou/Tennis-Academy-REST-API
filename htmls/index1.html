Welcome

Welcome to the Tennis Academy! If you are looking for an exciting tennis coaching experience in SW London for your children in the UK or for an amazing tennis experience overseas for the whole family, the Annabel Croft Tennis Academy can offer you a range of fantastic opportunities to play the game, meet new friends and develop a love for playing tennis. Annabel Croft is the former British No.1 and current Sky Sports tennis presenter and you can sample the methods that she used in her tennis career and recommends for every budding tennis player! Featuring a range of high energy drills and exercises that are delivered in small coaching groups, the coaching programme offers players of all abilities a chance to reach their potential.





Services

Kids Tennis

Our Instructors have a lot of experience coaching childern. They are fully qualified and are happy to transfer their skills to young childern to help them achieve the best they can in tennis.

Adult Tennis

We have all kinds of levels for adults so that anyone can join whenever they want. Adult sessions are held daily in the afternoon so that work does not come in the way of training
